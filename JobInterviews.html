Interview Tips

Click a square to see focused guidance for each stage of your interview journey.

Before the Interview

  • Research the company and role thoroughly.
  • Prepare STAR stories for common behavioral questions.
  • Practice your introduction and technical answers aloud.
  • Test your interview setup (internet, mic, camera).
  • Prepare thoughtful questions to ask at the end.

During the Interview

  • Listen carefully and clarify questions if needed.
  • Use structured answers and think aloud for technical tasks.
  • Show enthusiasm and highlight teamwork skills.
  • Balance detail with conciseness.
  • Maintain positive body language and engagement.

After the Interview

  • Send a thank-you email within 24 hours.
  • Note down what went well and areas to improve.

  • Keep applying and preparing for other opportunities.
  • Stay positive and view each interview as practice.
