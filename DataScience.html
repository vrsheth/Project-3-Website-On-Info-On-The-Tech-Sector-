Schools That Offer Data Science

The University of Calgary’s Data Science program equips students with a strong foundation in statistics, computer science, and mathematics while emphasizing practical applications in AI, machine learning, and big data analytics. Students gain hands-on experience with tools and programming languages commonly used in the field, preparing them to tackle real-world challenges. The program integrates interdisciplinary learning, offering opportunities to apply data science to areas like business, health, and engineering. With strong ties to Calgary’s growing tech sector, it provides pathways for co-ops, internships, and industry collaboration. Graduates are prepared for roles in data analysis, AI development, and research, or for further study at the graduate level.

Mount Royal University offers a Bachelor of Science in Data Science, one of the first of its kind in Western Canada. The program blends mathematics, statistics, and computer science with practical training in programming, data analysis, and machine learning. Students learn how to work with large datasets, build predictive models, and apply data science to solve complex problems across industries. Emphasis is placed on ethical data use, critical thinking, and communication of results. The program also provides opportunities for research, applied projects, and industry connections in Calgary’s tech and business sectors. Graduates are prepared for careers in analytics, AI, and data-driven decision-making, or for advanced study.
